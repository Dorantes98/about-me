Assignment 2, Oct 28

Challenges

I think the most difficult parts of this assignment was getting the size and spacing right for all the individual parts of the page. I took it step by step by first starting with the header of the page, then the filter bar, and finally the posts. One of the challenges in this was using padding vs margin and knowing when to use which one. A lot of the time I was trying both to see which one worked better. Another challenge I had was resizing the page. At first the posts on the page wouldn't stay a fixed size and would resize according to the page size. I fixed this by making sure the individual posts were a fixed size and making sure the layout was a proper grid.

What I Learned

A lot of the styling of font, font-weight, colors, background colors, etc. were things I feel I had a solid grasp on while working on the project. I learned a lot through trial and error about padding, gaps, and margins. Another thing I learned about was grid-template-columns and how to use it to fix my resizing issues.

Resources

I had to rewatch Oct 16 lecture about shadows in order to get the shadows of the "add item" button down right. Another resource I used was CSS-Grid tutorial. This helped me with my resizing of the webpage.

I didn't have to google anything for this assignment. I was able to find everything I needed through the lecture notes and rewatching videos. I did look through some of the CSS reading materials on the course website to work through some challenges like the grid and resizing.
